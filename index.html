Netherlands Virtual Observatory

Welcome to the home page of the Netherlands Virtual Observatory (NLVO). On those pages we introduce the Virtual Observatory, and the work that happens within the Dutch community to improve the standards, and increase the amount of astronomical data shared through the VO protocols. The Netherlands has a very large and divers astronomy community. The image of the right shows an example of how to combine public Dutch data in the VO by showing M101 in the LoTSS with contours from the WENSS.

The Virtual Observatory

The Virtual Observatory (VO) is the vision that astronomical datasets and other resources should work as a seamless whole. Many projects and data centres worldwide are working towards this goal. They gather in the International Virtal Observatory Alliance (IVOA). NLVO is one of those members.
